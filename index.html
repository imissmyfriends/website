We are a two person part-time indie game studio based in India, building Fishbowl—a narrative driven pixel art game.



Fishbowl is a heartfelt slice-of-life story where you spend a month at home as Alo. Navigate life in your early twenties, experience the nostalgia of childhood, work your way through your dreams and loss. All while balancing between a hectic job and home chores, keeping in touch with loved ones, rediscovering memories and making meaningful decisions to know yourself better, one day at a time.
